LazyMatch

A cozy match-3 puzzler.

Swap and match colourful tiles, chain satisfying cascades, and clear puzzles at your own pace. No timers, no pressure — just a relaxing loop you can pick up and put down whenever you like.

LazyMatch is a portrait puzzle game for Android, built around a hand-crafted set of levels with a clean, enamel-pin aesthetic and zero fluff. Pure match-3 joy, nothing else.
